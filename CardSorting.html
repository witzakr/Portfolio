Card Sorting

This assignment involved Card Counting, a technique utilized for designing and evaluating the information architecture of a website. In a card sorting session, participants organize various topics into categories that align with their understanding, and they may also suggest labels for these groups. Our group project incorporated this card sorting method as part of our research to gauge opinions on the most effective way to structure a portfolio website. It was an engaging assignment that provided valuable insights, and I'm pleased to share that we successfully completed it.
