Testing

Testing our project was a crucial step to ensure everything worked well. We employed various methods to assess our website, including:
5-second test: Users had 5 seconds to look at the website and remember the basic layout.
A/B method: Showed two different pages to users and asked for their impressions on each.
Guiding test: Instructed users to try using a specific function of the website.
First impression test: Showed the website to users and asked for their immediate thoughts.
We engaged 6 participants, that were students, to assist with the testing, making the process enjoyable. The most satisfying part was when everything worked smoothly, but even when people provided feedback on areas for improvement, it was valuable. We utilized their suggestions to enhance our project. Additionally, we prepared a comprehensive user test report summarizing how the tests went, adding an extra layer of insight into the overall performance and user experience. Overall, our testing phase was successful, and we gained valuable insights from it.
