Guess the number game

Description
×

I recently had a Front-End development assignment that involved creating a game called "Guess the Number." The main objective was pretty straightforward: players had to guess a randomly generated number between 1 and 100 within a limited amount of attempts to win the game.
The task wasn't too challenging for me. I didn't need to do much research because I could rely on what I've learned in my classes and my previous experience. It was more about applying my existing knowledge to build the game.
Even though it wasn't a particularly difficult assignment, I enjoyed working on it. The final result turned out well, and I'm satisfied with how the game functions. Overall, it was a fun and rewarding project!
