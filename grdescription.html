Our group project involves working in teams of 5 or 6 people to improve a client's website. The main objective is to redo the website according to specific guidelines provided by the client. Good communication between our group and the client is crucial for the success of the project. The project is divided into smaller parts called sprints, each focusing on a specific aspect like user research, communication, design, and development. Each of these topics will be expanded in their own sections of the portfolio.
