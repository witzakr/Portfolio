Communication

Effective communication is essential for successful projects, and it involves two main aspects: communication within the team and communication with clients and stakeholders. Within our team, we used WhatsApp, Discord, and Google Docs. WhatsApp was handy for exchanging quick information, while Discord helped us share links and files efficiently. Google Docs served as central storage for non-code work, while our coding projects were stored on our university's GitLab. When it came to communicating with our clients and stakeholders, we created presentations for each project phase. These presentations showcased our progress and the work we had completed.
As for the client, we used email communication and provided a tutorial to guide them on how to use the final website. We also created a short pitch presenting our group overview.
