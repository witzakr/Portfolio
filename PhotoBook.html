The main goal was to create a photo book, and it included a series of photography tasks, each with its own set of instructions. Moreover, for each task, we had to draw conclusions and incorporate noticeable effects into the photos. To tackle this, I made use of a Canon camera that I borrowed from ISSD. The project presented a significant challenge as it involved diverse tasks, demanding creativity and sometimes on-the-spot improvisation. I had to find subjects to photograph and experiment with various camera settings and programs. However, in the end, I managed to complete the project.
To make my life easier, I used blurb extensions, which helped me with planning out the layout and making a clear page division. While it might sound like it just does the whole work for you, I had to learn how this extension works and that was not an easy process.
